Activities

Upcoming Events:

International Workshop on Multimedia Applications (IWMA 2019): February 12-16, 2019 (Theme: Smart City) IWMA 2019 aims to discuss the advances and applications of multimedia technologies like: Image processing and computer vision, computer graphics, Human Computer Interaction, Machine Learning and Pattern Recognition, Internet-of-Things etc. in the context of smart city initiatives. The workshop will focus on issues related, but not limited to, visual surveillance, heritage conservation and structural health monitoring. Confirmed speakers are: Prof. Narendra Ahuja, University of Illinois Prof. Phalguni Gupta, IIT Kanpur Dr. Ujjwal Bhattacharya, ISI Kolkata Dr. Sumantra Dutta Roy, IIT Delhi Dr. Anoop Namboodri, IIIT Hyderabad Dr. Anil Tiwari, IIT Jodhpur IBM and MATLAB are supporting the workshop.
