Activities

International Workshop on Multimedia Applications (IWMA 2019): February 12-16, 2019 (Theme: Smart City) IWMA 2019 aims to discuss the advances and applications of multimedia technologies like:

  • Image processing and computer vision,
  • computer graphics,
  • Human Computer Interaction,
  • Machine Learning and Pattern Recognition,
  • Internet-of-Things etc. in the context of smart city initiatives.
The workshop will focus on issues related, but not limited to, visual surveillance, heritage conservation and structural health monitoring. Confirmed speakers are:
  • Prof. Narendra Ahuja, University of Illinois
  • Prof. Shantanu Choudhary, CEERI Pilani (IIT Delhi)
  • Prof. Rahul Banerjee, LNMIIT
  • Dr. Phalguni Gupta, NITTR, Kolkata (IIT Kanpur)
  • Dr. Ujjwal Bhattacharya, ISI Kolkata
  • Dr. Anoop Namboodiri, IIIT Hyderabad
  • Dr. Sumantra Dutta Roy, IIT Delhi
  • Dr. Anil Tiwari, IIT Jodhpur
  • Dr. K.S. Raju, CSIR-CEERI Pilani
IBM, MATLAB and NIVIDIA are Industry Partners of the workshop.
